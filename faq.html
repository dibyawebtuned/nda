Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about our services, support workers, and NDIS support.

Disable Help is a support bridge. We help NDIS participants and families find or change support workers and providers, and we also assist NDIS providers with admin, tech and compliance tasks so they can focus more on care.

No. Disable Help is not a registered NDIS provider, planner or plan manager. We work alongside your existing supports by helping you understand your options, connect with suitable providers and manage some of the background admin.

We support: NDIS participants and families looking for a support worker or wanting to change providers, NDIS service providers and support worker teams who need help with admin, scheduling, invoicing support and reporting

Yes, where possible we provide support in different languages and with a strong focus on cultural understanding. We know that feeling “seen” and respected makes a big difference to how safe and supported people feel.

We’re based in Perth and work with participants and providers in Melbourne and other parts of Australia. If you’re unsure whether we can help in your area, just contact us and we’ll let you know.
