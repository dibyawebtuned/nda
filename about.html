About Disable Help

Connecting NDIS Participants with the Right Support – Fast

Disable Help is an independent service that bridges the gap between people with disability, NDIS support workers, and service providers. From our base in Perth, we support participants in Melbourne and across Australia to find support workers who truly understand their needs – while also helping providers with the admin, tech and compliance tasks that keep their services running smoothly. Whether you’re searching for the right support worker, want to change providers, or need help managing NDIS paperwork and workflows, we’re here to make the process simpler, clearer and more human.
